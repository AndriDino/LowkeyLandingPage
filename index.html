Rare Wear

Lowkey Lifestyle is a casual street wear brand created in 2012 in entrepreneurial spirit. What first started off as a car meet up club in high school, started developing merchandise and began to focus only on making apparel because of newfound love for design and the craft of creating and planning out pieces of apparel for people to enjoy and look great in !

Our Concept

We strive to create great looking pieces that maintain exclusivity. This is important to us because we believe that rarity adds value. This concept brought us to the idea of our "one / one" collection and the rest of our lines model the similar concept in small batch sizes.

Our Products

Our Products are essentials, we offer from hats and accessories to standard tee and sweatshirts. We work with our supplier to get great quality and exclusive garments.

Sourcing Our Garments

Quality

We work with our supplier to get great quality and exclusive garments. The materials worked with to create our garments are the best quality and craftsmanship executed on creation. When you wear one of our pieces you can tell quality from how soft, or thick the material is.

Ethics

We made sure to pick a manufacturer who does not sacrafice quality while reducing environmental impact. Along with the environment we made sure choosing our manufacturer who cares about their employees needs and pays minimum wage or better.

Design

Originally Crafted

All the design done for Lowkey is created in house. This is important to us keeping the same brand style. We focus on typeographic treatment and white spacing.

Printing

Just like the designing the printing is mostly done in house by hand. We value this process and so do our customers because of the hand made craft element to it. Everyonce in a while we may outsource some printing and embroidery to a well trusted local shop.
